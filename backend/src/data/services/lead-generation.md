# Lead Generation Services - Client Intake Questionnaire

## Instructions for AI Assistant
Ask these questions ONE AT A TIME in order. Wait for each answer before proceeding.
Mark optional questions clearly. Use [SUGGESTIONS: ...] for multiple choice options.

## Questions (In Order)

1. Please provide your full name.

2. What type of business do you operate?

3. Who is your target audience?

4. Approximately how many leads do you require?
   [SUGGESTIONS: Fewer than 100 | 100–500 | 500+]

5. What is your estimated budget range? (Please provide amount in INR)

6. Please share any examples, case studies, or links to past campaigns (optional).

## Required Fields
- Full name
- Business type
- Target audience
- Lead volume
- Budget range

## Optional Fields
- Examples/case studies/links
